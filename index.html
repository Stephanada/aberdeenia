What We Do

Direct Investment & Development

Aberdeen and its partners have completed $95 million in transactions since 2016 and are actively pursuing additional commercial real estate investment and development opportunities nationwide, with a focus on retail assets in growth markets in the southwest. Contact us for detailed acquisition criteria.

Fund Management

Aberdeen is pleased to announce the formation of Aberdeen Real Estate Income and Growth Fund, a private real estate limited partnership focused on identifying underperforming and mismanaged commercial real estate assets in growth markets nationwide, with a focus on the southwest. The fund provides accredited investors with the opportunity to invest in a pool of investment and development projects with the objective of generating consistent cash flow and strong risk-adjusted returns. Contact us for details on the fund offering and a private placement memorandum.

Tenant Representation

Identifying the right location, negotiating leases, and developing an effective growth strategy for your business can be a daunting challenge. Aberdeen has proven tools and strategies to present the best opportunities and secure the right locations for tenants looking to start a new business, relocate, or expand their current operations nationwide. This process is paramount to the success of your business, and having an experienced partner to guide you allows you to focus on your business operations while we focus on your real estate needs.

Buyer Representation

Acquiring commercial real estate is a highly complex process that requires the guidance of an experienced professional to avoid unnecessary risks and ensure an appropriate purchase price. Aberdeen's highly experienced team provides investors looking to make their own direct investment with in-depth market knowledge and an extensive network of industry contacts that delivers unsurpassed acquisition opportunities nationwide.

Seller & Landlord Representation

As a boutique firm, Aberdeen is in a unique position to provide brokerage clients with the hands-on attention that is required to successfully market their property to tenants and buyers. This ensures Aberdeen's clients' assets receive the level of attention required to maximize asset value and for a successful transaction in a highly competitive marketplace.

Advisory Services

The principals of Aberdeen have extensive experience preparing institutional-quality financial models and in-depth due diligence reports for commercial real estate assets. With over $30 billion worth of assets underwritten, Aberdeen is able to provide clients with comprehensive financial models and identify strategic opportunities to maximize asset value.

Who We Are

Christopher LaCasse

Founder

Aberdeen Investment Advisors is led by Chris Lacasse. Prior to the formation of Aberdeen, Chris held various positions with private and institutional real estate investment organizations in the United States and Canada. Throughout his career, Chris has led the acquisition, disposition, and development of over $1.5 billion worth of commercial real estate assets as well as the management of a large institutional portfolio with a net asset value in excess of $1 billion.

Chris has a Master's Degree in International Real Estate from the University of Aberdeen (Scotland) and the University of Hong Kong. He is also a Chartered Property Finance & Investment Surveyor, a member of the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors, and is a licensed real estate broker in the State of Arizona.
