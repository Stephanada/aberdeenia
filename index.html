In Depth

How We Work

01

Direct Investment & Development

Aberdeen and its partners have completed $95 million in transactions since 2016 and are actively pursuing additional commercial real estate investment and development opportunities nationwide, with a focus on retail assets in growth markets in the southwest. Contact us for detailed acquisition criteria.

02

Fund Management

Aberdeen Real Estate Income and Growth Fund is a private limited partnership focused on identifying underperforming and mismanaged commercial real estate assets in growth markets nationwide, with a focus on the southwest. The fund provides accredited investors with the opportunity to invest in a diversified pool of projects, targeting consistent cash flow and strong risk-adjusted returns. Contact us for the fund offering and private placement memorandum.

03

Tenant Representation

Identifying the right location, negotiating leases, and developing an effective growth strategy can be a daunting challenge. Aberdeen has proven tools and strategies to present the best opportunities and secure the right locations for tenants looking to start a new business, relocate, or expand nationwide. Having an experienced partner allows you to focus on operations while we focus on your real estate needs.

04

Buyer Representation

Acquiring commercial real estate is a highly complex process that requires experienced guidance to avoid unnecessary risk and ensure an appropriate purchase price. Aberdeen's team delivers in-depth market knowledge and an extensive network of contacts, providing unsurpassed acquisition opportunities for investors making direct placements nationwide.

05

Seller & Landlord Representation

As a boutique firm, Aberdeen provides brokerage clients with the hands-on attention required to successfully market their property to tenants and buyers. This ensures every asset receives the care needed to maximise value and achieve a successful transaction in a highly competitive marketplace.

06

Advisory Services

Aberdeen's principals have extensive experience preparing institutional-quality financial models and in-depth due diligence reports. With over $30 billion in assets underwritten, Aberdeen delivers comprehensive financial models and identifies strategic opportunities to maximise asset value for clients at every stage.

Who We Are

The Team

Christopher LaCasse

Founder & Principal

Prior to founding Aberdeen, Chris held positions with private and institutional real estate investment organizations in the United States and Canada. Throughout his career, Chris has led the acquisition, disposition, and development of over $1.5 billion in commercial real estate assets, as well as the management of an institutional portfolio with a net asset value exceeding $1 billion.

Chris holds a Master's Degree in International Real Estate from the University of Aberdeen (Scotland) and the University of Hong Kong. He is a Chartered Property Finance & Investment Surveyor, a member of the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors (RICS), and a licensed real estate broker in Arizona.
